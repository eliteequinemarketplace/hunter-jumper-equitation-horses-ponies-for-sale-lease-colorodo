Colorado and the Mountain West represent one of the fastest-growing hunter jumper markets in the United States — a region that has seen significant growth in equestrian infrastructure, competition quality, and buyer/seller activity over the past decade. Elite Equine Marketplace lists hunter jumper horses from Colorado and Mountain West sellers, connecting buyers with quality horses in a market that is increasingly competitive and well-regarded.

Colorado's Growing Hunter Jumper Community

Colorado's equestrian community has expanded significantly — with world-class training facilities, an active competition calendar, and a growing population of serious hunter jumper riders and programs across the Front Range and beyond. Denver and the surrounding area are home to multiple top-level training facilities, and Colorado-based horses increasingly compete at the highest levels of national hunter jumper competition.

Value in the Mountain West Market

One of the most compelling aspects of the Colorado and Mountain West horse market is value — quality hunter jumper horses that would command premium prices in Florida or California are often available at more accessible price points from Colorado sellers. For buyers willing to travel or arrange transportation, the Mountain West market frequently offers exceptional quality at below-coastal-market prices.
